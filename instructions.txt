Title: Amazing Fluffy Waffles
Waffles recipe:
1. Combine dry ingredients in mixing bowl
2. Combine egg yolks, milk, and melted butter in separate bowl
3. Whisk egg whites until fluffy
4. Mix dry ingredients with wet ingredients
5. Fold in beaten egg whites
6. Laddle batter onto hot waffle iron
7. Let's begin!
